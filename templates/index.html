Workflow

What happens after launch

  1. SHUCK3R opens a browser window and waits for your site login.
  2. It walks your favorites pages and builds a queue before downloads start.
  3. yt-dlp saves files locally while the session page reports counts and recent log lines.

Constraints

Archive-aware and local-first

Operational notes

  • PornHub: Downloads from your favorites playlist (requires login).
  • xHamster: Downloads from your favorites page (requires login).
  • Videos save under your user profile (Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\HamsterScraper\downloads; override with HAMSTER_USER_DATA).
  • Library skip: PornHub resolves each title via yt-dlp. xHamster uses titles from the page. Very different filenames may still download.

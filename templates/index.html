Workflow

What happens after launch

  1. SHUCK3R opens a browser window and waits for your site login.
  2. It walks your favorites pages and builds a queue before downloads start.
  3. yt-dlp saves files locally while the session page reports counts and recent log lines.

Constraints

Archive-aware and local-first

Operational notes

  • PornHub: Chrome scrolls your favourites, then downloads each video via page flashvars (requires login).
  • xHamster: Downloads from your favorites page (requires login).
  • Pixiv: Bookmarks or a single artwork URL; ugoira export as GIF (Chrome login; optional FlareSolverr in Settings).
  • Files save under downloads/ph, downloads/xh, or downloads/pixiv in your profile (Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\HamsterScraper\downloads\…).
  • Library skip: PornHub reads titles from each video page. xHamster uses titles from the page. Very different filenames may still download.
